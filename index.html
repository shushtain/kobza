About Kobza

Kobza is a private voluntary initiative made under the MIT license. It means you can share these materials freely, edit them, use them for personal and commercial goals.

I have studied... I somehow learned English on my own. It was frustrating. I always wanted to organize that chaos.

Please be advised that there might be some errors in the functionality of the website, as well as some mistakes in the materials. You can always share your thoughts with me, but for now I build it on my own.
