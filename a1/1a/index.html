Practice

Complete the sentences.

  1. Are you a teacher? — Yes, I am.
  2. Are we free now? — Yes, go.
  3. I am not Maria. Maria is my mom.
  4. Are you not from Brazil? — No, I'm Chilean.
  5. How old are you? — We are six and seven.
  6. You are so kind. Thank you!
